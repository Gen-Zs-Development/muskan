About Us

Dedicated to supporting Gen Z's mental health journey

Our Mission

MindGen was founded with a clear mission: to provide accessible, effective mental health support specifically designed for Generation Z. We understand the unique challenges faced by today's youth, from digital pressures to academic stress, social media anxiety to identity formation.

Our team of mental health professionals combines expertise with genuine understanding of Gen Z's experiences. We offer a range of services tailored to meet your needs, whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, stress, or simply seeking personal growth.

At MindGen, we believe that mental health is just as important as physical health. We're committed to breaking down stigmas and creating a safe space where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and concerns without judgment.

Mental Health Self-Assessment

Check your mental well-being with these 10 important questions

Please answer each question honestly based on how you've been feeling over the past two weeks. This assessment is not a substitute for professional medical advice, but it can help you understand your current mental state.

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed by your daily responsibilities?

2. Do you find it difficult to enjoy activities that used to bring you pleasure?

3. How would you rate your sleep quality over the past month?

4. Do you often experience physical symptoms like headaches or stomach issues when stressed?

5. How often do you find yourself comparing your life to others on social media?

6. Do you feel comfortable expressing your emotions to others?

7. How would you describe your ability to focus on tasks?

8. Do you have a support system you can rely on during difficult times?

9. How often do you practice self-care activities?

10. Have you noticed significant changes in your appetite or weight recently?
